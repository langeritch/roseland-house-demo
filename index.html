Our Services

Everything taken care of

WiFi

Free wireless internet access in all our rooms. A business training and meeting room seats 16 people, with a private lounge for informal meetings. Valet and laundry service on request.

Transfers

We make use of a concierge service for airport transfers. These must be booked in advance with your flight details and time of arrival at King Shaka airport.

Security

Security for our guests is a priority. Our property has a perimeter wall with additional electric fencing, and the entrance gate is accessed by your own remote control.

Other services offered Parking, television, swimming, walking, kitchen, laundry, delivery and collection of dry cleaning, posting, faxing and e-mailing, delicious home-made South African dinners by arrangement, and business breakfasts by arrangement.
